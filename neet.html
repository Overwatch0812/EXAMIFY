The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Undergraduate) or NEET (UG), formerly the All India Pre-Medical Test (AIPMT), is an all India pre-medical entrance test for students who wish to pursue undergraduate medical (MBBS), dental (BDS) and AYUSH (BAMS, BUMS, BHMS, etc.) courses in government and private institutions in India and also, for those intending to pursue primary medical qualification abroad.[3] The exam is conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA), which provides the results to the Directorate General of Health Services under Ministry of Health and Family Welfare and State Counselling Authorities for seat allocation.

DETAILS:-

Frequency:-Once in a year
Mode of examination:-Offline
Duration of examination:-200 minutes(3 hr,20 min)
Official website:-NEET OFFICIAL SITE
Courses:-MBBS,BDS, BSc Nursing, AYUSH, Veterinary, and other medical/paramedical courses.
Subject:-Physics, Chemistry, Biology
Question Type:-Multiple Choice Questions
